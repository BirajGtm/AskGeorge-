INVESTMENT IN GBSP CENTRE CORP. Pursuant to a Joint Venture Agreement dated February 12, 2004, the College has a 50% interest in GBSP Centre Corp., a joint venture corporation providing facilities for performances as well as for education and instruction in theatre, dance, music and the related arts. The joint venture has a December 31 fiscal year end. The College’s equity share of the joint venture from April 1, 2013 to March 31, 2014 has been included in these financial statements using the modified equity method.
