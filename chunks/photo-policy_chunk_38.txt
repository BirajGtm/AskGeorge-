Photo Policy The Childcare Centre is a demonstration laboratory centre for the School of Early Childhood at George Brown College. The centre is used as a practical training centre for students in the School of Early Childhood and Diploma Certificate Programs. To support this training, students, Staff and faculty will be required to take photographs or videos to be used for educational purposes. These photos are only to be used for the purpose of the student’s academic experiences (Learning Stories, Documenting Curriculum, Class Assignments etc.) Photos are not to be scanned, emailed or posted on any social media or web sites to ensure children’s privacy. Student photos are only to be taken on the Centre’s designated camera’s and IPad and not on a student’s personal device. The centre will facilitate printing directly or at a designated printing facility. Revised: July 2017 1
