The Foundation’s primary goal is to raise funds for the College’s capital initiatives and programs and for student awards. The College provided the Foundation with financial support during the year ended March 31, 2012 of $1,003,889 (2011 - $476,780) for operations and $1,739,229 (2011 - $610,200) for fundraising projects and expenses.
